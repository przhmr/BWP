Community Involvement

Giving back to our community is a priority at Bluewater Pro Pool Services. As members of the Pool & Hot Tube, we’re proud to be sponsors of the Step Into Swim Program and contribute each year financially. We’re also part of the USA Swim Foundation and the Stand by Me Foundation.

Step Into Swim Program

Our team at Bluewater Pro Pool Services is dedicated to making sure every child has an opportunity for success; that’s why we contribute to the Step Into Swim Program. We’re happy to be part of this wonderful program that teaches children from different backgrounds how to swim as a life-saving skill while having fun in the water.

Learn More

USA Swim Foundation

The Bluewater Pro Pool Services team is proud to support the USA Swimming Foundation. Our contribution helps provide direct athlete support for our nation’s top swimmers while allowing them to focus on their swimming goals. As a donor, you can help athletes reach their potential and earn their spot atop the podium by contributing to their rigorous training schedule.

Stand By Me Foundation

At Bluewater Pro Pool Services, we’re a company that believes in helping adults, children, and the disabled. We’re part of the Stand by Me Foundation, which provides infrastructure, competencies, and medical follow-up to adult swimmers so they can prepare for international meetings. They also develop swimming sports activities for people with disabilities.

We Truly Care About Our Local Community

We at Bluewater Pro Pool Services are glad to be part of and contribute towards making a difference in the lives of all those who want to learn how to swim. With our help, we hope that people will experience water safety and enjoy it!
