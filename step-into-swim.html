Step Into Swim

At Bluewater Pro Pool Services, our commitment goes above and beyond! We’re certificated by the Pool & Hot Tub Alliance (PHTA), and we’re dedicated to ongoing education through their “Step into Swim” program.

Our goal is to help everyone live healthier lives by enjoying aquatic activities like swimming!

Step Into Swim is an initiative created by PHTA to help one million more people swim in 10 years. We’re also dedicated to ensuring that American children have access to swim lessons so they can learn about water safety as well!

Let’s Create More Swimmers Together

Why not learn how to swim? Drowning is a huge risk for children, and taking lessons can reduce that by 88%. It’s never too late or early! Moreover, swimming is a great way to stay healthy and happy. It not only has the power to heal our bodies but also provides us with safety and peace when it’s time for rest.
